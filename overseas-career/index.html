About Us


Since its inception in 2001, Overseas Career has been helping the Indian students achieve their dream of studying MBBS from a reputed university abroad. Overseas Career is a reputed leading consultancy based in New Delhi, India, helping the aspiring minds to pursue a lucrative higher education and career abroad in some of the leading medical colleges of Russia, Kyrgyzstan, Philippines, Ukraine, China, Poland, and Georgia. We have our dedicated associates all over India, enabling students from across the country to experience our high-end overseas MBBS admission services. Guiding the young minds to find the best colleges in the leading countries which fit their skill set and budget requirements is our main motive as we extend our specialized services.

We offer the highest quality of educational counseling to the students to make their dream of pursuing a successful career in MBBS come true. With complete dedication and constant support, we help the students choose their desired MBBS college or university in the leading countries to help them grow into successful professionals. You can be assured of making the rightmost career option by availing our services at Overseas Career.
