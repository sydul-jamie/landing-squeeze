About Us


It’s time to break the routine. Time to explore, engage with, and embrace the unknown.
It’s time to challenge your perspectives, indulge your passions and discover new ones with Nexus.

ince its inception, Nexus Educational Services Pvt. Ltd. has been offering quality guidance to the aspiring students who wish to pursue their higher studies abroad. We are one of the most highly reputed and sought after overseas education and counseling service providers in India. With more than a decade of experience in this field, we have served thousands of students to successfully get admission into the world-renowned universities in UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Europe, New Zealand, and so more.

Overseas education is both an aspiration as well as a major decision in life. Owing to the immense technicalities involved in the admission procedure into overseas universities, it can be overwhelming for the students and their parents as well. Lack of proper information with respect to the admission and the visa procedure can result into a big time chaos. The core conviction of Nexus Educational Services Pvt. Ltd. is to help the students and their respective parents overcome these hurdles as we offer expert guidance and consultation services for getting admission into the dream college abroad. Our high-end educational service approach ensures the successful admission into the desired university abroad as per the unique skill set and capabilities of individual students.

Mission

To establish as a pioneer in the field of offering top-notch overseas educational services to enable the young students achieve their dreams.

Vision

To incorporate the ideology of skilled learning by creating a strong foundation in the education industry and also attaining the repute of being a superlative provider of high-end educational services.

History


An ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company, Nexus Educational Service Pvt. Ltd. has garnered the respect to be a leading name in offering high-end overseas admission and counseling services. Since its inception, we are aimed at helping out the young aspirants to achieve their dream of studying in a reputed overseas college or university of their dreams. Nexus Educational Services Pvt. Ltd. takes pride in being a certified agent of the British Council Education, BMQR ISO 9001 certified, and accredited by AIAO-BAR.

With a remarkable history and track record of serving thousands of students across the nation, we continue to extend our specialized services in the field of overseas education.
